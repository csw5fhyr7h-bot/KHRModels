How the number is made. A pitcher's strikeouts-per-batter is shrunk toward the league rate by how many batters he's actually faced (small samples get pulled in hard), then upgraded by Savant whiff% when you've pasted it — whiff is the single most predictive strikeout skill. The opponent's team strikeout rate scales the matchup, expected batters faced comes from his innings-per-start, and the projection converts to 6+ / 8+ probabilities through a Poisson distribution — a genuine probability law, not a heuristic. The archive saves each day's board; after games finish, "Fill actuals" pulls the real strikeout totals from the box scores and the calibration panel tracks the model's true error over time. Constants are editable in Model settings. A decision aid, not betting advice.
