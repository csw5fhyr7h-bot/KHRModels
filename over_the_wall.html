How the number is made (skill first, environment capped). Season HR/PA is shrunk toward the league rate by sample size, then blended 60/40 with an expected rate built from barrel% and hard-hit% — the stable skills. Park, platoon, pitcher, temperature and wind multiply on top, but the combined environment factor is capped at ±40%, so a hot night at a bandbox can lift a hitter — it can never put a whole lineup above a real slugger. Per-game probability converts the rate through expected plate appearances by lineup slot. Every constant is editable in Model settings. Projections are a decision aid, not betting advice.
